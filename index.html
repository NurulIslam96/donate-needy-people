Our Donate Plan

You can donate to needy people for various purposes like Education, Food or for their Shelter. You can choose any plan from below to support needy people.

Food for a family

You can donate money for the food of a needy family. We take extra care in buying healthy food and deliver it to the family in right time.

Shelter for a family

This option is for the family who are desperately in need of shelter. We provide them adequate shelter along with basic necessities.

Blood for a patient

You can also donate blood to a needy patient. A Whole blood donation requires 8-10 minutes while you will be lying down.
